Date: 8/02/2026
Meeting Type: Product Demo

People:

Discussion Summary:
David demonstrated TaskMaster Pro and explained how it helps teams track work.
Sarah explained that ABC Company currently uses email and Excel for task tracking.

David showed how tasks can be created, assigned to team members, and given deadlines.
He confirmed that files such as documents and images can be attached to tasks, with a limit of 100MB per file.

David explained that automatic email reminders are included, while SMS reminders cost an additional $50 per month.

Sarah requested weekly reports for management.
David confirmed that TaskMaster Pro automatically sends weekly PDF reports by email every Monday.

David offered a free trial of TaskMaster Pro for 10 users for 7 days.
For ABC Company’s team of 15 users, David extended the trial period to 10 days.

Pricing options were discussed:
- Basic plan: $100/month for up to 20 users
- Pro plan: $150/month including SMS reminders and advanced reports

Sarah expressed concern about the Pro plan price.
David offered a discounted first month at $120, with a decision required within two weeks.

Support options were discussed:
- Email support is included
- Phone support costs an additional $30/month

David agreed to include phone support in the total price of $150 per month.

Training options were discussed:
- Free training videos are available

Sarah confirmed that training videos are sufficient for now.
